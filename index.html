GEM TD

Build a maze of gems. Each wave you place 5 random gems — then keep just one. The rest crystallize into rock walls that lengthen the creeps' path.

◆ Place 5 gems each wave by tapping the field.
◆ After the wave, keep 1 — the other 4 become rock walls.
◆ Combine two matching gems to upgrade their quality, or follow a recipe for a powerful special gem.
◆ Leave creeps a path to the exit — you can't wall them off completely.
